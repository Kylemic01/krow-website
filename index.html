The Program

What happens during 6 months

Three phases. A defined finish line. A business that works without you.

1
Dependency Audit + Systems Foundation (Months 1–2)
Map every point of owner dependency. Build the first SOPs. Identify the five things only you know how to do. Then begin transferring them.
2
People + Operations (Months 3–4)
Hire or restructure so a manager runs day-to-day without your involvement. Build the training and accountability systems around them.
3
Revenue Without the Owner (Months 5–6)
Lead generation running without you. Financials clean. Business is buyer-ready or you simply keep it and enjoy the life it gives back.
Common Questions

Answered honestly

Is this right for my industry?
If you're doing $1M–$4M per year and your business depends on you daily, yes. HVAC, cleaning, chiropractic, med spa, recruitment, logistics, accounting, agencies. If you have 5+ employees and a signed lease, we can almost certainly help.
What if I'm not planning to sell?
The program works whether you ever sell or not. Removing yourself as the bottleneck gives you the choice. Most owners come in wanting an exit and stay because they've got their life back.
How much is it?
The group program starts at $4,500. The 1-1 premium is $12,000–$15,000. On a $400K EBITDA business, fixing owner dependency adds $600K–$1.2M in exit value. The audit is free that's where we start.
I've tried to delegate before and it didn't work.
The problem is almost never the person you hired it's the absence of a documented system for them to run. Phase 1 fixes that before Phase 2 touches any people decisions.
What if I don't get results?
Attend every session and complete the Phase 1 actions in the first 30 days. If you don't walk away knowing exactly what to fix first I work with you free until you do. No loopholes.
