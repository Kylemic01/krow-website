Common Questions

Answered honestly

Is this right for my type of firm?
If you run a tax, bookkeeping, CPA, fractional CFO, or payroll practice and manual work is eating hours every week, yes. This isn't about replacing judgment — it's about removing the repetitive parts around it.
What if I'm not sure AI is worth the risk yet?
That's exactly what the quiz and audit are for. We show you the specific number before you commit to anything — no pitch, no pressure, just the math.
How much does it cost?
Placeholder pricing — starts around $2,500 for the core engagement, with a fractional-team option for larger firms. The audit itself is free — that's where we start, and the price only comes up once you've seen your number.
I've tried ChatGPT or Copilot before and it didn't really change anything.
Most firms that try AI on their own use it ad hoc — one person, one browser tab, no process behind it. The difference here is a specific workflow, trained on your firm, that your whole team can rely on the same way every time.
What about client confidentiality?
This is the most common objection, and a fair one. Every recommendation here assumes paid-tier tools only, client names stripped before anything is processed, and a clear AI-use clause in your engagement letter — not "just paste it into whatever's free."
What if I don't get results?
Attend every session and complete the Phase 1 actions in the first 30 days. If you don't walk away knowing exactly what to automate first I work with you free until you do. No loopholes.
