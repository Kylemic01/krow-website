How this is calculated

This estimate starts from your annual SDE (owner's discretionary earnings — your real take-home, not just "profit" on paper) and applies a multiple based on factors that actually move what buyers pay for landscaping businesses: how much revenue is recurring vs. one-off, how dependent the business is on you personally, how long it's been operating, and team size.

Landscaping businesses typically sell for somewhere between 2x and 5x SDE. Recurring maintenance contracts pull that multiple up significantly — businesses with heavy recurring revenue routinely sell for close to double the multiple of project-only operations, because the revenue is predictable instead of something that has to be re-won every job.
